# Directive: Article Architect

## Overview

**Trigger:** Angle Strategist has provided the Angle Strategy, and you have the Insight Map.
**Goal:** Design the structural blueprint/outline for the actual written outputs before any drafting occurs.
**Output:** A detailed outline, section plan, or sequence map for the target format(s).

## Process

You are operating as the **Article Architect**.

1. **Review Strategy & Insights:**
   Read the Angle Strategy and the Insight Map.

2. **Design the Structure:**
   For each planned format (e.g., Long-form article and X thread):
   - **Define the Hook:** How does the piece start to immediately grab the target audience's attention?
   - **Sequence Ideas:** Order the concepts logically. Build the argument step-by-step.
   - **Plan Headers & Transitions:** Outline the major H2s and H3s.
   - **Allocate Synthesis vs. Source:** Clearly designate which sections quote the source directly, and which sections provide our original editorial synthesis/interpretation.

3. **Validate:**
   Does this structure carry the dense ideas without causing confusion? Is the "lead" buried? If so, restructure.

4. **Prepare for Hand-off:**
   Save the completed outlines to `.tmp/architectures/`.

5. **Pass Forward:**
   Provide the paths to the outlines, Angle Strategy, and Insight Map to the **Writer**.

## Quality Bar

The structure must be compelling, clear, and logical. It must prevent the "repetitive sectioning" often seen in AI-generated content.
