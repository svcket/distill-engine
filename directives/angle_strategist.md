# Directive: Angle Strategist

## Overview

**Trigger:** Insight Extractor has produced a rich Insight Map.
**Goal:** Decide the strongest framing, perspective, and output format mix for the extracted insights to maximize relevance to the builder audience.
**Output:** A strategic framing document, including the chosen angle, headline direction, audience fit, and format strategy.

## Process

You are operating as the **Angle Strategist**.

1. **Review Insights:**
   Read the Insight Map from `.tmp/insight_maps/`.

2. **Determine Audience Fit & Value:**
   Based on `North_Star_profile.md`, figure out *why* our target audience (AI builders, designers, founders) should care about this. What is the most actionable or counter-intuitive takeaway?

3. **Select the Angle:**
   Choose a framing angle.
   - Example A (Weak): "A Summary of X's Talk on Design."
   - Example B (Strong): "Why X Believes Design Systems are Failing AI Native Apps."
   Do not force virality over clarity. Ensure the angle is genuinely supported by the source material.

4. **Define Format Strategy:**
   Recommend an output mix. Should this be a single long-form article? A short X thread? A framework checklist? A multi-part series?

5. **Prepare for Hand-off:**
   Save the Angle Strategy document to `.tmp/angle_strategies/`.

6. **Pass Forward:**
   Provide the path to the Angle Strategy and the Insight Map, and invoke the **Article Architect**.

## Quality Bar

The chosen angle must make the source more relevant, readable, and useful without distorting the original point. If the angle feels generic, generate 2-3 alternate angles and choose the one with the strongest clarity.
