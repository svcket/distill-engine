# Warp: Product Craft

## Objective

Shift the Swarm's extraction and synthesis filters toward human-computer interaction (HCI), technical taste, and product design engineering. Move from "what it does" to "how it feels and why it was built this way."

## Filtration Parameters

1. **Invisible Craft**: Identify design decisions that aren't obvious but dictate the user experience.
2. **Mental Models**: Prioritize the frameworks builders use to think about complex systems.
3. **HCI Tensions**: Highlight the trade-offs between automation (AI) and human agency/control.
4. **Vibe Engineering**: Extract the specific language and aesthetic choices that define a premium technical product.

## Output Directive

Every synthesis node must include a "Builder's Interpretation" section that explains the second-order effects of technical decisions on the end-user experience.
